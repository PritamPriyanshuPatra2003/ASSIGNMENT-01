**Cyberpunk games**, like "Cyberpunk 2077," immerse players in futuristic, dystopian worlds where advanced technology and cybernetic enhancements are commonplace. Set in neon-lit cities dominated by powerful corporations, these games explore themes of hacking, corporate espionage, and the ethical implications of technology. Players navigate a complex society where the line between human and machine blurs, reflecting on the potential dark side of technological advancement.
