Cyberpunk 2077 is a 2020 action role-playing video game developed by CD Projekt Red, and published by CD Projekt, and based on Mike Pondsmith's Cyberpunk tabletop game series. The plot is set in the fictional metropolis of Night City, California, within the dystopian Cyberpunk universe.


In October, sales of “Cyberpunk 2077” crossed 25 million units, according to CD Projekt. Investment bank Stifel estimates that amount equates to roughly $770 million, making it a solid hit and on par with first-year unit sales of the most popular games from Activision Blizzard's Call of Duty franchise.
